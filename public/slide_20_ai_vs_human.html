AI VS HUMAN: WHO DOES WHAT?

AI (Gemini)

The Engine
  • Generates code (Boilerplate)
  • Finds syntax errors
  • Offers solutions to problems
  • Refactors code quickly

You (Developer)

The Pilot
  • Determines direction (Prompting)
  • Checks security
  • Evaluates UX/UI quality
  • Responsible for the final result
AI is your Co-pilot, not an Autopilot. You lead the process.
