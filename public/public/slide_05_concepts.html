GENAI, LLM AND PROMPTING

GenAI

Generative AI – models that create new content (text, code, images). It's not just a search, it's creation.

LLM

Large Language Model – a large neural network that understands and generates text based on probabilities.

Prompting

Basically an instruction.

The key to success is clarity, context and constraints.
